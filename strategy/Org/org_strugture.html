Assumptions & Generalization:
  • Organization hierarchy is canonical: Company → Business Group → ISU → Sub-ISU; cardinalities are typically 1..*.
  • Sub-ISUs serve Customer Accounts in a *..* manner (multiple accounts per Sub-ISU and vice-versa).
  • Programs are sponsored by Customer Accounts and may serve multiple accounts; accounts can sponsor multiple programs (*..*).
  • Programs decompose into multiple Projects (1..*).
  • Persons can play multiple Roles across time, and Roles can be held by multiple Persons (*..*).
  • Projects are staffed by People and support multiple Value Chain stages (e.g., Claims), both *..*.
  • Sub-ISUs focus on specific Value Chain stages (e.g., Clinical UM) in a *..* fashion.
  • Enterprise Architects govern ISUs; Solution Architects design Programs (both *..*).
