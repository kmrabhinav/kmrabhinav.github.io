Learning Aid for SDD & Harness Engineering

Welcome to your interactive curriculum dashboard. This application is a dedicated learning resource aid designed specifically for developers, architects, and technical leaders who want to master the paradigms of Spec-Driven Development (SDD) and Harness Engineering.

Why this Aid is Relevant

As autonomous software development loops replace manual text edits, traditional "vibe coding" (ad-hoc conversational prompting) triggers severe context decay and undocumented, fragile design assumptions. This map structures your upskilling around the industry's two most dependable engineering responses:

  • Spec-Driven Development: Elevating your engineering workflow upstream. You master authoring clear, machine-readable specifications (using precise rulesets like EARS syntax) which act as version-controlled contracts. The agent then handles syntactic assembly as a compilable build artifact.
  • Harness Engineering: Structuring the scaffolding (Guides like AGENTS.md, and Sensors like automated evaluators and code-graph linters) surrounding a model to achieve production reliability.

How to Navigate Your Learning Path

  • Phase Trajectory: The curriculum flows horizontally through 7 distinct development segments, connecting conceptual foundations and local CLI tooling directly to enterprise scaling matrices, supply-chain provenance, and evaluation pipelines.
  • Modular Discovery: Click on any curriculum node to check its core parameters—including complete resource descriptions, duration, accessibility, cost structures, and directional logic. Drag modules to personalize your layout, or search modules dynamically using the text bar above.
